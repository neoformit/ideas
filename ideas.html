Good ideas for stuff

transparent.org

  • Online resource to encourage corporate transparency
  • Anonymous submissions are vetted for integrity
  • Users can search for any company or institution to find dirt (or perhaps complements)
  • May also include a forum for discussion and insight into public perception

unharrass.org

  • Online resource of legal advice for harassment victims
  • Pro-bono legal service for victims
  • Sue companies, institutions and people for income
  • Shake-down organisations to reform their policies and procedures

Profanity bar

  • An LED text bar to display single-line message
  • Customized message by editing on SD card
  • 3x3 button board allows quick deployment of 9 different messages
  • Mount in rear windscreen to communicate better with inferior road-users
